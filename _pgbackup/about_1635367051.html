Our Vision

Engineers and architects will communicate their opinions and designs with precision and speed. They will be recognized as the nimble thought leaders of the building industry.

Our Mission

The building repair and restoration industry has lagged behind most others in its adoption of modern technology. In particular, forensic engineers and architects are among the most complacent, commonly using decades-old processes and tools for communication that are regarded as obsolete in parallel professional service industries.

FEC will drive the building repair and restoration industry forward by utilizing and developing latest generation technology from 3D imaging to UAVs to client/project-specific web applications. We will pursue process improvements from inception to close-out, always striving to provide bespoke expert service with revolutionary speed.

Our Values

Our core values make us unique in our industry, and we consciously return to them for guidance in all decisions, strategic to tactical. We are not a group of individuals that cooperate when convenient; instead, we are a tightly knit team that comes together daily to share workload, experience, and support. We know the whole is greater than its parts.

Integrity

The building repair and restoration industry presents many opportunities to profit from dishonest behavior, especially after catastrophes. We embrace our ethical obligation to lead by example with honesty and transparency.

Purposeful Speed

Moving with purposeful speed means filling all moments with pragmatic action; we conduct our investigations sensibly and realistically, prioritizing practial rather than theoretical considerations. Desktop analysis certainly has its value, especially as a starting point, but empirical measurements and real-world observations are the strongest evidence.

We move fast in the office and in the field. We recognize time is valuable for everyone, but time on site is particularly precious for us. When conducting a site investigation, we arrive prepared with backups and contingency plans.
